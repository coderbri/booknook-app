## Overview

BookNook is a mobile application where readers can discover, share, and organize book recommendations. Users can post reviews with star ratings and photos, browse an infinite-scroll feed of community picks, and personalize their experience with color themes.

---

## Features

- **Authentication** — Secure sign-up and login with authorization
- **Home Feed** — Infinite scrolling dashboard of book recommendations
- **Create a Post** — Share a recommendation with a title, star rating, photo, and caption
- **Profile Page** — View and manage (delete) your own posts
- **Color Themes** — Personalize the app's look and feel
